## Research question

Can Osprey-inspired optimisation improve global maximum-power-point tracking when partial shading creates multiple peaks in the PV power curve?

## System model

The Simulink system includes a PV model, MPPT controller, boost converter, load, measurements, power calculation and optimisation logic.

## Evaluation

PSO, Osprey Optimisation and a modified Osprey method were compared for extracted power, convergence, stability, tracking efficiency and behaviour under multiple irradiance patterns.

## Interdisciplinary value

The project connects an EEE foundation with intelligent optimisation, data interpretation and applied algorithm research—forming a bridge to later AI&DSE work.

## Roadmap

Hardware validation, rapidly changing weather tests, sensor noise, larger arrays, battery integration and embedded controller implementation.
