## Learning system

Each entry explains the tool category, purpose, workflow, safe laboratory example, output interpretation, defensive relevance, limitations and ethical-use boundary.

## Laboratory scope

Exercises are restricted to controlled systems such as Kali Linux, Metasploitable, Windows virtual machines and OWASP Juice Shop on isolated networks.

## Why it matters

The initiative converts daily practice into a searchable knowledge base, interview revision resource, classroom material and public record of disciplined learning.

## Expansion

Future series can cover SOC tools, Windows events, MITRE ATT&CK techniques, Python security scripts and incident-response exercises.
