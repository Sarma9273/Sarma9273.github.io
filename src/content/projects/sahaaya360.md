## Product vision

Small institutions often manage complaints, assets and safety work through disconnected notebooks, messages and spreadsheets. Sahaaya360 explores a unified, affordable operations layer built first with accessible Google Workspace tools.

## MVP modules

Institutions, tickets, assets, vendors, safety checks, cyber incidents, DPDP readiness, monthly reports and form links.

## Ticket workflow

A form submission creates a structured record, generates a ticket ID, classifies the request, suggests an owner, assigns an SLA target and tracks status through resolution.

## Product lessons

The project required prioritising a demonstrable MVP, designing data structures, handling Apps Script execution contexts, enabling APIs, protecting backups and preparing proof material for potential users.

## Roadmap

Role-based access, dedicated web and mobile interfaces, notifications, AI-assisted classification, escalations, vendor performance, subscription plans and privacy-support features.
