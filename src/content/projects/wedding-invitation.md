## Design objective

Extend a traditional invitation into a mobile-friendly digital experience without exposing developer notes, source references or internal content instructions.

## Experience decisions

Each event has a dedicated thumbnail, description, date, time and venue. Family categories are organised clearly, navigation returns guests to useful content positions and light/dark themes maintain readable cards and text.

## Content operations

Drive-hosted source images are organised with predictable filenames so family members can update galleries without changing application logic.

## Roadmap

RSVP, maps, calendar links, multilingual content, reminders, guest-specific links and a protected gallery.
