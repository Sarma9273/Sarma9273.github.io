# Brand Guide

## Positioning

**Guru Charan Mavuduru**  
**SOC Analyst | AI-Driven Cybersecurity | Applied AI & Data Science**

Supporting identity:

> M.Tech AI&DSE student at IIT Patna, B.Tech EEE graduate, educator and practical project builder.

## Brand pillars

1. Practical security operations
2. AI that supports human judgement
3. Systems and workflow automation
4. Documented research and learning

## Visual direction

- Deep navy represents security and systems.
- Cyan represents telemetry, intelligence and forward motion.
- Violet is used sparingly for AI and research depth.
- Large headings are balanced by restrained cards and readable body text.
- The blazer photograph is the primary professional portrait.
- The checked-shirt photograph supports the About and personal-journey narrative.

## Writing voice

- Explain the problem before the tool.
- State the project status honestly.
- Include failures, limitations and future work.
- Avoid claiming production readiness for prototypes.
- Use first person for decisions and learning; use neutral language for technical explanations.
